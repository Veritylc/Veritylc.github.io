For Volunteers

Make a difference with Verity Law Collective. Volunteer your time, skills, or simply help spread the word!

How You Can Help

There are many ways to support our mission:

  • Legal Aid Support: Help with research, document preparation, and consultations.
  • Education & Outreach: Develop workshops and create informational resources.
  • Resource Development: Assist in creating legal guides and tools.
  • Administrative Support: Manage schedules, communications, and updates.
  • Spread the Word: Share our mission on social media or recommend our services to those in need.
