Legal Aid & Pro Bono Services

At Verity Law Collective, we strive to connect individuals with free or low-cost legal resources. Whether you are facing a civil, criminal, or family law issue, we are here to help guide you through the process and provide you with resources to access the support you need.

Free Legal Aid Services

If you are in need of legal assistance but cannot afford an attorney, several organizations provide free or reduced-fee services. These resources can help you navigate your legal issues without the financial burden.

  • Legal Aid Society - Offering free legal representation for low-income individuals. Visit Website
  • National Legal Aid & Defender Association - Connecting individuals with public defenders and legal assistance for criminal and civil cases. Visit Website
  • Pro Bono Net - A network of pro bono legal services across the country. Visit Website

Pro Bono Legal Services

Many law firms and individual attorneys volunteer their time to provide free legal services for those in need. Below are some resources for finding pro bono attorneys.

  • American Bar Association - A directory of pro bono attorneys across the United States. Visit Website
  • Volunteer Lawyers Project - A network of pro bono lawyers dedicated to assisting low-income individuals. Visit Website

National Legal Aid Networks

If you are looking for legal aid on a national scale, these organizations provide resources and support to connect you to local services.

  • Legal Services Corporation - The largest funder of civil legal aid for low-income Americans. Visit Website
  • National Association of Pro Bono Professionals - Helping connect individuals with pro bono opportunities. Visit Website

Legal Assistance by Area of Law

Need legal help in a specific area of law? Here are some resources tailored to different legal issues.

Consumer Rights

  • Consumer Financial Protection Bureau - Information and resources for resolving consumer complaints. Visit Website
  • National Consumer Law Center - Advocating for low-income consumers in areas like debt collection and foreclosure. Visit Website

Family Law

  • National Domestic Violence Hotline - Resources for those facing domestic violence, including access to free legal services. Visit Website
  • Child Welfare Information Gateway - Resources for child custody, child support, and family law. Visit Website

Criminal Law

  • Innocence Project - Legal assistance for those wrongfully convicted. Visit Website
  • American Civil Liberties Union - Legal support for criminal justice reform. Visit Website

Immigration Law

  • Immigrant Legal Resource Center - Providing educational resources for immigrants and their legal rights. Visit Website
  • American Immigration Lawyers Association - Helping connect individuals with experienced immigration attorneys. Visit Website
