Free Legal Aid and Pro Bono Services

Many individuals face legal issues but cannot afford traditional legal services. Below are resources that offer free legal assistance:

  • Legal Aid Organizations: Local legal aid services offering free assistance. Contact your regional provider for services in your area.
  • Pro Bono Legal Assistance: Law firms and attorneys offering free services for qualifying individuals. Find resources in your area.
  • National Legal Aid Networks: National organizations such as the Legal Services Corporation that provide access to legal support services across the country.

Legal Information and Guides

These educational resources provide you with the basics of various legal topics:

  • Consumer Rights: Learn about your rights when it comes to consumer transactions, fraud, and disputes.
  • Family Law: Information on divorce, child custody, and domestic violence.
  • Criminal Law: Understand the criminal justice system, your rights when arrested, and available legal defenses.
  • Employment Law: Get the basics on workplace rights, wage disputes, and discrimination issues.
  • Housing Law: Know your tenant rights, the eviction process, and resolving landlord disputes.
  • Immigration Law: Resources for individuals navigating immigration and visa issues.

Self-Help Tools

Access tools and templates to assist with basic legal processes:

  • Legal Forms: Download common legal forms such as power of attorney, wills, and rental agreements.
  • Legal Rights Calculator: A self-assessment tool to understand if you qualify for specific legal assistance programs.
  • Case Evaluation Tools: Get guidance on how to evaluate the strength of your case before contacting an attorney.
